About me

Get to know me and my work – experienced, professional, transparent.

Philipp Höfinghoff

Managing Director & Master Roofer

I was born in North Rhine-Westphalia and am now based in Berlin. As a master roofer with over ten years of professional experience, I have developed a deep understanding of the challenges and subtleties of the roofing trade.

With PlanDach Höfinghoff, I pursue a clear mission: to bring the highest quality, transparency and innovation to the roofing and sheet metal industry. I offer independent assessments, professional planning and individual consulting – always with the goal of ensuring long-term security and value appreciation of my clients' properties.

My approach combines traditional craftsmanship with modern project management and digital services. With our Online Roof Check, I enable fast, precise evaluations – whether on-site in Berlin or nationwide online. This allows my clients to benefit from efficient processes, clear communication and tailored solutions.

I want to do more than just secure roofs – I want to set standards, build trust and give every client the confidence that their project is in the best hands. My standard is simple: every project should be executed the way I would expect it done for my own home.
