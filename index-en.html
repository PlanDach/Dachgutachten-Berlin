Frequently Asked Questions

Do you also provide assessments nationwide?▼
Yes, I provide independent assessments throughout Germany – both on-site and digitally via the Online Roof Check.
How does the Online Roof Check work?▼
You send me photos and information about your roof. I review them and provide a professional assessment with recommendations.
Do you also handle site supervision?▼
Yes, I offer site supervision and construction monitoring to ensure professional and correct execution.
Can I sign a maintenance contract?▼
Yes, I offer regular roof maintenance individually tailored to your building.
What does an assessment cost?▼
Costs depend on the type and scope. After an initial consultation you will receive a transparent quote.
Can I get an appointment at short notice?▼
In urgent cases I will arrange an appointment as quickly as possible – best to reach out by phone.
