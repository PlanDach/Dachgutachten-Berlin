Our Services

Professional roofing expertise – independent, precise and reliable. Tailored solutions for every project.

What We Offer

With over ten years of hands-on experience in roofing and construction, PlanDach Höfinghoff delivers a comprehensive range of expert services. From the first inspection to the final sign-off – we are your independent, professional partner at every stage of your project.

01

Roof & Building Inspections

On-site & Remote Nationwide Independent

A thorough inspection is the foundation of every sound roofing decision. We carry out detailed assessments of flat roofs, pitched roofs and building envelopes – identifying defects, water ingress, structural weaknesses and workmanship issues with precision. Our inspections are fully independent, giving you an unbiased picture of the actual condition of your property.

Request an inspection
02

Technical Reports & Expert Assessments

Legally Recognised Insurance Claims Dispute Resolution

Our expert reports are prepared to the highest professional standards and are suitable for use in insurance claims, legal proceedings and construction disputes. Each report is clearly structured, evidence-based and written in a language that is accessible to clients and legal professionals alike. We document defects with photographic evidence and provide actionable recommendations.

Request a report
03

Planning & Technical Project Support

Concept to Execution Tender Preparation Cost Planning

Good planning is the key to a successful project. We support you from the initial feasibility study through to detailed technical planning and tender documentation. Our expertise covers flat roof systems, pitched roof structures, waterproofing and drainage. We prepare precise specifications and bills of quantities that form a solid basis for contractor selection and cost control.

Discuss your project
04

Construction Supervision & Quality Control

On-site Monitoring Defect Management Final Inspection

Ensuring that roofing work is executed correctly requires expert eyes on site. We monitor construction progress, verify compliance with approved plans and applicable standards, and identify deviations before they become costly problems. Our supervision service includes regular site visits, photographic documentation, defect logs and professional final acceptance inspections – protecting your investment from start to finish.

Remote Consulting & Digital Roof Check

Nationwide Fast Turnaround Digital First

Through our Digital Roof Check, you submit photos and information about your roof online and receive a structured expert assessment with clear recommendations – quickly and without the need for an on-site visit. Ideal for property owners, landlords and buyers seeking a fast, reliable second opinion before making decisions.
